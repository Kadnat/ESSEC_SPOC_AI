## ðŸŽ¥ Video Production Tips

### Pacing
- **Speak clearly and confidently** - avoid rushing
- **Pause after key points** - let information sink in
- **Use transitions smoothly** - "Now let's see...", "Notice how...", "Here's the breakthrough..."

### Screen Recording
- **Full screen application** - hide desktop clutter
- **Zoom interface elements** when showing details (match percentages, skills)
- **Pre-load CVs** to avoid fumbling with files
- **Practice transitions** between tabs

### Visual Flow
1. Start wide (homepage overview)
2. Zoom in (specific features)
3. Repeat for each CV
4. End wide (architecture/conclusion)

### Engagement Techniques
- **Use numbers:** "20 job offers", "87% match", "1,584 job roles"
- **Highlight surprises:** "Here's the breakthrough", "Notice the difference"
- **Show real results:** "This is not mock data - these are real jobs"
- **Emphasize speed:** "In just 5 seconds..."
